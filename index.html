A

Universe

of

Information

In 1990 the eminent theoretical physicist John Archibald Wheeler suggested the idea that all physical things are fundamentally information-theoretic. Atoms, energy, space-time and everything else exists as answers to binary, yes-no questions, that is measurements. Furthermore, he speculated that physical reality is created by observers in the universe.

This idea has been approached in different forms by philosophers throughout history.
